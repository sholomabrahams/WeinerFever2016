How to Manually Edit Play Time

  • The time above will be displayed in the format m:SS.ms. When minutes or milliseconds are 0, they will not show.
  • Each input will take an input of up to 2 characters.
  • You need not enter a value into every input, but if you do, either minutes or milliseconds must be 0
  • After you enter two characters into a field (or one into minutes), your cursor will automatically jump to the next input (or to the submit button after MS).
  • You can use the and keys to raise/lower the numbers in a field.
